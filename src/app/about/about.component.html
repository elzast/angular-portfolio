About the Developer

Hello and welcome! My name is Elijah and I'm a Software Developer with a strong interest in design and accessibility.

I "unofficially" began my journey into Software Development as a hobbyist designer. Little did I know tweaking blog pages and profiles would lead me into the exciting and highly dynamic world of tech.

Once I had learned there was a huge, unfulfilled need for making websites and applications more accessible I knew I needed to be a part of the change I wanted to see in tech and enrolled in a coding bootcamp to start implementing the changes I wanted to see.

This site is a collection of my skills and projects throughout time. Continue to follow up for updates and feel free to contact me to connect, collaborate or for work opportunities.
